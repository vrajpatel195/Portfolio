Mediapp

  • Tech Stack: Flutter, Dart, Firebase
  • Github URL: Project Link

MediApp simplifies the process of booking doctor appointments by offering a user-friendly platform accessible to both patients and doctors. With convenient login options for patients and doctors, users can effortlessly navigate the app to manage their healthcare needs.

Patients can easily schedule appointments with their preferred doctors directly through the app, while doctors can efficiently manage their schedules and patient information upon logging in. MediApp streamlines the healthcare experience for both patients and doctors, providing a seamless and convenient solution for appointment booking and management.
